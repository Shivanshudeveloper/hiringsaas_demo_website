CNSS Calculator

The CNSS, or Caisse Nationale de Sécurité Sociale, is Morocco's National Social Security Fund founded in 1972. Primarily serving private-sector employees and self-employed individuals, it plays a vital role in ensuring social protection for workers and their families.

At Actif, we give advisory service for salaried employees for the best format of their CNSS contributions, making sure they use their tax allowance to maximum and get maximum benefit. Please try our inhouse-developed CNSS Calculator and ask for a quote to prepare your monthly payroll.
